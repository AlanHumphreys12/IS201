Who's That?

Your new best friend.

Born in Texas, Raised in Utah, and Half Venezuelan; Alan is as fun and genuine as a person can be. With an extensive work history and a whole slew of hobbies and interests, Alan is the kind of person you'll always want around.

Talented.

He has participated in: Show Choir, Ballroom, Hip Hop, Concert Choir, Musical Theater, Student Government, Football, H.O.P.E Squad, and many other groups over the span of his life. Anything you want to do, he's up for it.

Intelligent.

Alan started to read 500+ page young adult novels at the age of 7. Mostly fantasy books, but as he got older he began to have a certain love for books on psychology, philosophy, and the scriptures. With him, you'll never run out of things to talk about.

Nerdier than you can imagine.

A jack of all trades, Alan dabbles in just about every fandom or interest you can think of. And if you have an interest he hasn't pursued, he would love to learn from and with you! Nothing gives him more joy than seeing the passion of others.
